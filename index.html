About

Dr. Whitehead is a veteran educator with almost 30 years of experience in the field. After her recent retirement, Dr. Whitehead accepted her new assignment to mentor, model and support educational institutions and clients into greater success. She has earned multiple degrees during her career in education. She has a Bachelors in Science Education, a Masters' in Counseling and Personnel Services, a Specialist in Educational Administration and a Ph.D. in Educational Administration.

She hails from the University of Southern Mississippi. Her career includes being a school teacher, school counselor, a school administrator, a college professor, and School Counseling Program Coordinator at the University of Southern Mississippi. Dr. Whitehead has earned license and certifications as National Certified Counselor, National Certified School Counselor, Licensed Professional Counselor and a Licensed Professional Counselor - Supervisor. She is a member of the Mississippi Counseling Association and the Mississippi Licensed Professional Counselor Association.

She enjoys sharing her knowledge and encouraging others. Her experiences include mentoring students, teachers, administrators, and college students. Through speaking engagements, consulting, life coaching and counseling, Dr. Whitehead uses her life experiences and expertise to lead others to Become the Best Version of Themselves!

Insightful Solutions was conceived as the result of a multitude of experiences that Dr. Whitehead encountered in her professional, academic and personal life. As an educator with almost 30 years of experience, she hopes to fulfill her purpose by collaborating with those around her and sharing her knowledge, skills, and insight to move her clients towards desired outcomes. Her volition is supported by multiple degrees, certifications, and intestinal fortitude that has helped to galvanize so many in her career. Dr. Whitehead’s multifaceted approach to situations is done professionally and with fidelity to honor her unwavering desire to serve others.

Let me help you discover the best version of you. Through life we experience many tests. These tests help to develop the character and fortitude that will help carry you to fulfill your destiny. Each of us was created with a purpose. No matter the circumstances encountered in life, we must learn to continue to move forward one step at a time, one day at a time. It is pertinent that we show up emotionally, physically, and mentally healthy for ourselves andthose around us. A healthy mind, body and spirit is the key to surviving the many challenges that we experience in life.My journey is not the one I thought I would be living at this point inmy life. I am a witness to doing the work to heal from trauma and pain. I had to come, in order to overcome, so that I could become all that I was meant to be. I believe in “Being the Best Version of You.”

Services

Coaching - Dr. Whitehead’s passion is to help others by setting goals and implementing strategies to successfully transition into new levels of life. Whatever major life transition is being experienced, we offer guidance and support to identify next steps to successfully navigate these stages in life. We all experience life transitions such as going to college. Self-discovery, marriage, divorce, retirement, etc. How we manage these, sometimes unexpected, stages can determine the level of success
Counseling - Services are available to individuals and families. The objective of Insightful Solutions is to provide the highest quality counseling services in this region. IS’ goals are to promote healing and growth with their clients by staying client centered and attentive to the clients’ needs throughout their time in service toachieve the best individual client outcomes
Consulting - Our goals are to build and guide our clients with integrity by approaching individual challenges experienced to bring about positive change. Insightful Solutions is committed to a collaborative relationship with our clients that leads to new insights, valuable resolutions, and improved performance.
